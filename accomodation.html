Accomodation.

We offer pubstay accommodation which is clean, cosy and affordable. We are perfectly situated within walking distance to town, 3km drive to the Tweed River Regional Art Gallery and 10 mins drive to the base of Mt Warning which is the core of an ancient extinct volcano.

Our rooms are suited for travellers and backpackers. We also regularly host travelling workmen and sales reps as well.

Our rooms have recently been renovated and are presented clean and serviced daily. There are a combination of rooms available. The rooms are situated upstairs from the pub and have a large balcony available to sit quietly and enjoy a cup of coffee and take in the view over the river. Alternatively you can join the many characters downstairs for what will be a memorable experience.
