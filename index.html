Hotel.

Relaxing on the wide open balcony overlooking the beautiful Tweed River in Murwillumbah, the Riverview Hotel offers a picturesque setting for you to enjoy a drink as well as the best pub food around.

The Riv is ‘The’ place to catch up with friends over lunch or dinner, with Budd Park and the World Heritage Rainforest tourist information centre just next door.

Bar & Bistro.

There is a wide selection of local and international beers with 16 on tap, including the locally handcrafted Stone & Wood range.

The Riv Bar and Bistro has something for everyone and a focus on quailty food at affordable prices. There is an extensive range of traditional pub dishes with gluten free options, a very popular children’s menu, and a light meal selection.

Fun & Games.

A well laid out TAB, Keno and Gaming lounge are available for your fun. We also have a darts board and pool table for a bit of competitive fun against your mates!

Be entertained with Live music, raffles and live sports action on the big screens.

Accomodation.

We offer pubstay accommodation which is clean, cosy and affordable. We are perfectly situated within walking distance to town, 3km drive to the Tweed River Regional Art Gallery and 10 mins drive to the base of Mt Warning which is the core of an ancient extinct volcano.

Our rooms are suited for travellers and backpackers. We also regularly host travelling workmen and sales reps as well.

Courtesy Bus

Once you’ve called in for a few drinks with friends, listened to some live music, had a feed and grabbed some takeaways from the bottlemart we’ll even give you a lift home with our Riverview Hotel courtesy bus. For bookings phone 0488 533 780
