Hotel.

Welcome to the Riverview Hotel, nestled on the bank of the beautiful Tweed River which runs right through the township of Murwillumbah located in the Northern Rivers of New South Wales Australia.

Our country pub is right next door to the World Heritage Rainforest tourist information centre where you can obtain visitor/car passes to our picturesque World heritage national parks which are just a short 20min drive from our Hotel.

Bar.

The best cold beer in town, are fully air-conditioned, boast a well laid out TAB, Keno and Gaming lounge area with smoking spaces permitted.

We also have a darts board and pool table for a bit of competitive fun!

Be entertained with Live music on Sundays, raffles on Thursday evenings and live sports action on the big screens.

Bistro.

'The Riv' Bar and Bistro with a wide open balcony overlooking the river. The Bistro menu has something for everyone and a focus on quailty food at affordable prices. There is an extensive range of pasta and pizza dishes with gluten free options, a very popular children’s menu, light meal selection and a traditional Bistro menu.

The lunch menu is also unbeatable for value. You can choose from fish, chicken, seafood, a classic burger or the old time favourite steak sanga all for just $12. With variety and prices like this, ‘The Riv’ is ‘The’ place to catch up with friends over lunch or dinner.

Accomodation.

We offer pubstay accommodation which is clean, cosy and affordable. We are perfectly situated within walking distance to town, 3km drive to the Tweed River Regional Art Gallery and 10 mins drive to the base of Mt Warning which is the core of an ancient extinct volcano.

Our rooms are suited for travellers and backpackers, we also regularly have workmen and travelling company reps staying with us as well.

Community.

Murwillumbah Football Club

Murwillumbah Football Club is one of the largest sporting clubs in the Murwillumbah District.

Murwillumbah Vulcans

The Riverview Hotel have been great supporters of this developing sport in the Tweed.
Every Saturday at about 2.30pm you will find club representatives down there selling tickets to Meat Tray raffles.
