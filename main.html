Hotel.

Welcome to the Riverview Hotel, nestled on the bank of the beautiful Tweed River which runs right through the township of Murwillumbah located in the Northern Rivers of New South Wales Australia.

Our country pub is right next door to the World Heritage Rainforest tourist information centre where you can obtain visitor/car passes to our picturesque World heritage national parks which are just a short 20min drive from our Hotel.

Bar.

The best cold beer in town, are fully air-conditioned, boast a well laid out TAB, Keno and Gaming lounge area with smoking spaces permitted.

We also have a darts board and pool table for a bit of competitive fun!

Be entertained with Live music on Sundays, raffles on Thursday evenings and live sports action on the big screens.
