Local. Seasonal. Tasty.

The Riv Bistro is more than your typical country pub kitchen. We not only serve the best pub classics, such as our Classic Beef Burger and Chicken Parmigiana, but also devine seasonal specials.

With all the hospitality of a comfortable country pub, we offer generous sizes, reasonable prices and locally sourced produce, enjoyed at the best location in town.
